Bar Charts

Bar charts are used to represent data as bars. Chart.js only allows the creation of dodged bar charts at the moment.

library(chartjs)
library(magrittr)
chartjs(row.names(mtcars), list(mpg = mtcars$mpg, cyl = mtcars$cyl)) %>% 
  barChart
