Bar Charts

Dodged bar charts

Chart.js only allows the creation of dodged bar charts at the moment.

library(chartjs)
library(magrittr)
chartjs(data = list(mpg = mtcars$mpg, cyl = mtcars$cyl), labels = row.names(mtcars)) %>% 
  barChart
